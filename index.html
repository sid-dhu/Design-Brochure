Layout & Structure

Page Architecture

How information is arranged communicates personality before anyone reads a word. Each wireframe below shows the skeleton of a real page structure.

Editorial Magazine
Text and imagery in asymmetric balance. Strong visual storytelling with deliberate white space and clear content hierarchy.
Full-Bleed Cinematic
Edge-to-edge hero sections with layered depth. Dramatic, immersive — photography or bold typography commands the screen.
Minimal Typographic
Words carry everything. Generous white space, perfect type hierarchy, almost no imagery. Radiates intellectual confidence.
Card Grid Modular
Content lives in cards — work samples, testimonials, articles. Scannable and organised. Scales well with rich content.
Narrative Scroll
The page tells a story as you scroll — chapters unfold sequentially. Perfect for brands with a journey arc.
Sidebar Biography
Fixed side panel anchors your identity (photo, nav, links) while the main canvas scrolls rich content.
One-Page Portfolio
Everything on a single scrollable canvas — masonry grid of work, quick bio, contact at the bottom. Efficient and punchy.
Split-Screen Hero
Left half is typography and CTA on dark background; right half is a full-bleed image. High impact, immediate contrast.
Long-Form Essay / Blog
Centred narrow column for deep reading. Optimised for long-form content, thought leadership, and newsletters.

Primary Objective

What is the #1 job of this website?

Being clear about the primary goal ensures the site doesn't try to do too much. Select one.

✓
Build Credibility
The site is a proof point. When someone Googles you, they should immediately understand why you're the right person.
✓
Generate Enquiries
Drive qualified leads — speaking invitations, consulting conversations, collaboration requests.
✓
Grow Audience
Capture attention, earn subscriptions, build a loyal following around your ideas.
✓
Sell a Product
A book, course, workshop, or programme. The site positions, educates, and converts visitors.
✓
Share Ideas
A living record of thinking. Essays, articles, talks — a mind made public, not a commercial vehicle.
✓
Everything, Balanced
Credibility + enquiries + audience + ideas, woven together. Achievable with the right architecture.
